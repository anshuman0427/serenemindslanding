Refund and Cancellation

Last updated: December 12, 2024

  • No Cancellation Option:
    • Clients do not have the option to cancel appointments once booked through the platform.
    • Professionals have the ability to reschedule their clients' appointments as needed.
  • Company-initiated Cancellation: The Company reserves the right to cancel or suspend accounts that violate the Terms and Conditions.

Refund Policy

  • Service Refunds: Payments made for individual sessions or appointments are non-refundable unless canceled by the mental health professional.
  • Technical Issues: If the Service is unavailable due to technical errors, users may request compensation, subject to verification.
  • Commission Policy: The 15% commission charged on the total booking amount is non-refundable, as it includes Razorpay processing fees, our service fee, and applicable GST.
  • Requesting a Refund: Refund requests can be made by contacting us via email at refunds@sereneminds.life with details of the transaction.

Processing of Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after approval and will be credited to the original payment method used.
